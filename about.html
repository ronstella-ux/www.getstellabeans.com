About Stella Market

Local beans. Bean-to-cup premium coffee. Better mornings. Less disposable waste.

Stella Market & Bean Co was built for offices that want better coffee without sending employees off-site for every latte or cold brew run.

Roasted in St. Charles

We highlight locally roasted beans from St. Charles and the broader St. Louis area whenever possible.

Built for modern offices

Premium espresso drinks, real milk, and real cold brew options with a polished breakroom look.

Sustainability mindset

Bean-to-cup systems help reduce dependence on disposable plastic k-cups and other single-serve waste.

Locally owned service

We serve offices in St. Charles, St. Louis, and the Greater St. Louis region with a local-first approach.
